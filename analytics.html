How It Works

1

Connect Your Wearable

Pair your MindPulse band or other compatible wearable device with our platform. The device continuously monitors vital biometric data including heart rate variability, skin conductance, and brain activity patterns.

2

AI Processes Your Data

Our sophisticated AI engine analyzes your biometric data in real-time, identifying patterns that indicate various mental states including stress, anxiety, focus, and calm. The system gets smarter over time as it learns your personal patterns.

3

Receive Personalized Insights

View your mental state trends and receive actionable insights through our intuitive dashboard. Understand your triggers and track progress over time.

4

Get Tailored Interventions

Based on your current mental state, the system offers scientifically-backed interventions to help you regulate emotions and reduce stress. These may include guided breathing exercises, meditation sessions, or journal prompts.

5

Access Support When Needed

During periods of elevated stress or anxiety, the system can automatically offer connections to our AI support chatbot or human wellness coaches for immediate assistance.
