Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of dedicated mental health professionals, TakeItEase was created with a simple but powerful mission: to make quality mental health care accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We're committed to removing barriers to mental healthcare and providing personalized, evidence-based treatment in a compassionate and supportive environment.

Our Vision

At TakeIt Ease, we believe in the power of relaxation and mindfulness to transform lives. Our mission is to provide accessible therapeutic solutions that help you disconnect from stress and reconnect with your inner peace. Founded in 2025, our team of wellness experts has developed a range of therapeutic approaches that cater to different needs and preferences..

Our Approach to Mental Wellness

At TakeItEase, we believe in holistic, personalized care that addresses the unique needs of each individual.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions with our licensed therapists using evidence-based approaches like CBT, DBT, and mindfulness to address depression, anxiety, trauma, and more.

Group Support

Therapeutic group sessions provide connection, shared experiences, and mutual support under the guidance of our experienced facilitators.

Medication Management

Our psychiatric providers work closely with you to find the right medication options when needed as part of a comprehensive treatment plan.

Holistic Wellness

Complementary services including meditation classes, nutrition counseling, and stress management techniques to support your overall wellbeing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of mental health conditions do you treat? +

We offer treatment for a wide range of conditions including anxiety, depression, PTSD, bipolar disorder, OCD, relationship issues, stress management, grief, and more. Our initial assessment will help determine the best approach for your specific needs.

Do you accept insurance? +

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans and offer sliding scale fees for those without insurance coverage. Our administrative team can verify your benefits before your first appointment and explain any potential costs.

How do I know if therapy is right for me? +

We offer complimentary 15-minute consultations to discuss your concerns and help determine if our services are a good fit for your needs. Therapy can benefit anyone looking to improve their mental wellbeing, whether you're dealing with a specific condition or simply seeking personal growth.

Do you offer virtual appointments? +

Yes, we offer secure telehealth sessions that provide the same quality care as in-person appointments. This option provides flexibility and accessibility for clients who prefer receiving care from home or have transportation challenges.
