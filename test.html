Mental Wellbeing Assessment

Complete this brief questionnaire to get insights into your current mental wellbeing. Please answer honestly for the most accurate results.

Note: This assessment is meant for educational purposes only and is not a diagnostic tool. Please consult with a healthcare professional for proper evaluation and guidance.

1. Over the past two weeks, how often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?

2. How often have you had little interest or pleasure in doing things you usually enjoy?

3. How often have you felt nervous, anxious, or on edge?

4. How would you rate your ability to handle stress in the last month?

5. How often have you had trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much?

6. How often have you felt tired or had little energy?

7. How often have you had trouble concentrating on things?

8. How would you rate your overall satisfaction with life currently?

9. How often do you feel overwhelmed by your responsibilities or daily tasks?

10. How often do you engage in activities that bring you joy or relaxation?
